My Recent Projects

PicturePerfect

PicturePerfect is an accountability mobile app in which users fill in a daily collage of images to create their Picture Perfect Day. Users upload one image per task, as to make that task a daily habit (up to 9 tasks per day). They can then share their completed daily collage with friends. View the accompanying gif for more clarification.

→ React Native, TypeScript, Expo Router, Supabase (Postgres)

BookBase

BookBase is a library management system that allows you to create your own digital library by keeping track of the books you own.

→ MongoDB, Express.js, JavaScript, Node.js, HTML, SCSS

Gift of Knowledge Academy

Gift of Knowledge Academy is an independent, nonprofit, K-2 school providing a firm foundation in literacy and math to low-income students. This site allows parents to enroll their students, learn more about the school, donate, etc.
