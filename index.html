About Me

I am a self-disciplined, hands-on software engineer who is dedicated to every phase of the application/website development process. From crafting the initial mock-up to adding those final, polished touches, I give every step my all. Complex coding challenges do not intimidate me, but instead drive me to uncover innovative solutions.

Though my path to becoming a full-stack developer was unconventional, I embraced the challenges it presented. I quickly learned that, at the end of the day, my success hinges on my own efforts. Soon enough, I had evolved from a student into a self-sufficient yet community-oriented software engineer (who is still learning new things every day). One of the ways I gave back to my community was by teaching children basic coding principles and sparking their interest in computer science.

If you're searching for a reliable team member who not only gets the job done but also brings a collaborative spirit to the table, you know where to look!
